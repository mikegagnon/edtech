Autodrone

You play the game by writing software that controls a simulated drone

https://mikegagnon.github.io/autodrone/

Student trial

I spent an hour with third graders at Thrive Elementary School in San Diego, about a year ago. We rushed through the game, due to time constraints, and overall I don't believe I was able to give the in-depth programming introduction I had hoped for.

However, students surprised me with their ingenuity for playfully hacking the software to make the drone blow up in entertaining ways.

Hope for the future of Autodrone

I would like to do another trial with students, but this time with much more generous time constraints.
