Gamer

Gamer is an AI Laboratory for JavaScript, designed to help novice students: (A) acquire programming practice, and (B) develop AIs based on the one of the most classic AI algorithms, MiniMax.

Documents: https://github.com/mikegagnon/gamer

Example game: https://mikegagnon.github.io/gamer/

Simplify

Gamer simplifies game development by providing graphics and controls. To create a game, the student only needs to develop the game logic itself.

Curriculum

Gamer also comes with curriculum. For example, here is a walk through that teaches you how to develop a Tic Tac Toe AI.
